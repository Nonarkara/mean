    A note on this book

    This is a small dictionary of words I have used wrong, and a smaller list of words I have used right. There are about one hundred entries in three of my languages and a few imports from elsewhere.

    You can read it in two ways. From A to Z, in roughly half an hour. Or one entry at a time — one a day, the way the calendar arranges itself anyway.

    Some entries are jokes. Some are arguments. Most are confessions.

    — Non · Bangkok · Shanghai · Boston
